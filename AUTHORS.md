## Contributors

Contributors are recognized under `community/contributors/`:
- `developers.md` — individual code contributors
- `companies.md` — organizations contributing engineering time
- `sponsors.md` — financial supporters
- `hall-of-fame.md` — long-standing high-impact contributors

To contribute, please read `CONTRIBUTING.md` and sign the Contributor License
Agreement (CLA) at the first pull request. Attribution follows the AUTHORS
convention: contributors are listed by full name and affiliation, with a
non-anonymous commit signature.
