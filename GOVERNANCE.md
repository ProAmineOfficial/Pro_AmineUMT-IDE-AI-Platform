# Project Governance

## Project Roles

- **Founder / Principal Architect** — Amine Saoud ibn al-Bashir. Retains
  final say on architectural direction, brand, and the ten normative
  standards. All Architecture Decision Records require founder approval.
- **CODEOWNERS** — maintainers with merge rights on specific directories.
  Defined in `.github/CODEOWNERS`.
- **Contributors** — anyone who has had at least one pull request merged.
- **Community members** — anyone participating in Discussions, Discord, or
  issues.

## Decision-Making

- **Day-to-day changes** (bug fixes, small features, documentation) — one
  CODEOWNER approval is sufficient.
- **Architectural changes** — new ADR required. Draft PR → 14-day comment
  period → founder decision.
- **Standards changes** — new revision of the affected standard under
  `standards/`. Two CODEOWNER approvals + founder approval.
- **License, NOTICE, TRADEMARKS** — founder decision only.

## Advancement

- Contributor → CODEOWNER: nominated by an existing CODEOWNER after
  sustained high-quality contribution to a specific directory.
- CODEOWNER → Steering Committee: reserved until the project reaches
  100 external contributors.

## Conflict Resolution

Technical disagreements are resolved by ADR. Interpersonal conflicts are
resolved under the Code of Conduct with escalation to the founder.

## Amendment

This document is amended by ADR. See `adr/` for the process.
